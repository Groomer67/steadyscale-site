Inside the app

A quick tour.

Ten screens — click, arrow, or swipe through to see the whole app.

Welcome

A short setup.

Onboarding takes about a minute. Pick how you want to track, choose a goal or reference weight, and set your weekly check-in day.

Tracking mode

Loss, Gain, or Monitor.

Pick the one that fits. The weekly trend works the same for all three — and you can switch any time without losing your history.

Goal weight

A goal you could maintain.

Pick a weight you could see yourself maintaining, not just reaching. BMI is shown as a reference, not a prescription.

Weekly rate

Pick a pace.

Choose any rate from 0.5 to 2.0 lb per week. Your rate sets a weekly target that stays constant throughout the week and updates after each check-in day.

Free trial

Free for 7 days.

No account, no marketing list, no upsells. Your data stays on your device and backs up to your iCloud. Cancel any time in iOS Settings.

Dashboard

Long term, this week, today.

Three cards show three time horizons: where you're heading, how this week's pace compares to plan, and today's daily average. The layout stays the same every morning — only the numbers update.

Chart

Noise into signal.

The chart layers raw weigh-ins, daily averages, and a 14-day rolling trend that filters out day-to-day noise. From your trend and chosen rate, it projects forward to your goal — so you can see when you'll get there.

Log

Log as often as you like.

Weigh in once a day or multiple times a day — whatever works for you. You can edit or backfill measurements any time, and unlikely entries get flagged so a typo doesn't throw off your trend.

Settings

Plan, preferences, and backup.

Your active plan lives in one card: edit, pause, or delete from there. Switching between Loss, Gain, and Monitor preserves your history — and deleted plans can be restored from Past plans.

Help

Every element explained.

The Help tab covers how the math works, how to read the chart, how your data is handled, and how to get in touch. Every part of the system is open.

Pricing

Your data

No accounts, no servers, no ads.

Your data belongs to you. We never see it. We've also removed every design pattern that adds pressure — no streaks, points, reminders, or motivational messages.
