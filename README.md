## About the app

SteadyScale is a weight tracking app for iOS. It averages multiple daily readings into a clean signal, forecasts your weekly check-in, and projects a finish date for your goal — all stored privately on your device and backed up to your iCloud.

**Key features:**
- Multiple daily readings averaged into a single daily value
- Fixed weekly check-in target that never shifts mid-week
- Short-range forecast with uncertainty cone
- Long-range projection to your goal date
- Three tracking modes: Loss, Gain, Monitor
- On-device storage with automatic iCloud backup — no account, no server, no ads
